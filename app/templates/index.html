What is Chess?

Chess is a board game payed between two players. Although early versions of chess have been around for centuries the most recent version of chess emerged during the 15th century in South Europe. Today chess is one of the most popular games in the world, played by millions of people.

Why Learn Openings?

Chess openings are a vital part of every players games. Openings are not only important to get you in a good position early in the game, but they can serve to set up your position for the rest of your game. All good chess players study openings and their respective variations. Simply put, knowing openings are an essential weapon in every chess players arsenal.
